Community Service

Community service has always been a big part of my life. In whatever way I can I always strive to make make a difference, big or small, in my community. This has led me to be involved in multiple service clubs and be chosen for leadership positions in them. These activities have also built up my experience and helped me develop my skills professionally as I learn how to communicate and coordinate activities with great organizational skills.

California Scholarship Federation - Activities Coordinator
This club is the largest student body on campus and is completely run by students. I have been involved since my middle school(CJSF, junior version) and got involved in highschool starting freshman year. Sophmore year I got chosen to activities coordinator. I coordinate volunteer activities and ensure everything runs smoothly. I also communicate frequently wirh local schools and event organizers who need our help. As a part of the club I have volunteered to help run events such as our school's robotics tournament, the Science Olympiad and Math Olympiad competitions.

Presidential Volunteer Service Award (2019) - Gold
This award is a national award given to people all across the United States to recognize them for their service. I was awarded the gold division award in 2019 for volunteering over 100 hours in one year.
Interact (Rotary sponsored club) - Intern Webmaster
Dougherty Valley Interact is part of District 5160 and is sponsored by the Danville Rotary club. I have been involved since freshman year of high school and am currently intern websmaster. Along with my webmaster responsibilities I coordinate service activities. This involves strong communication with the event coordinators and volunteers along with reliability and planning capabilities. Through this club I have volunteered to help the special ed children, assist with school events, and the renovation of the community.
